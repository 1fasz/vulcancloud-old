The Reality

Most Businesses Discover Their Backup Is Broken During a Disaster

Backups that run on schedules no one audits. Restores that have never been tested. Recovery plans that exist as a Word document no one has read since 2019. This describes the backup situation at most SMBs — and it's only visible when something catastrophic forces a recovery attempt.

Ransomware attacks, hardware failures, accidental deletions, and natural disasters don't schedule themselves. Your recovery capability needs to be tested before you need it — not during.

VulcanCloud manages your backup infrastructure end-to-end: automated jobs, integrity verification, off-site replication, and regular recovery testing — so you know exactly what your recovery looks like before it matters.

  • ✦Automated daily backups with integrity verification
  • ✦Off-site replication to geographically separate data center
  • ✦Defined RPO (Recovery Point Objective) targets
  • ✦Defined RTO (Recovery Time Objective) commitments
  • ✦Quarterly recovery testing with documented results

What RPO and RTO Mean for Your Business

Recovery Targets That Match Your Risk Tolerance

RPO is how much data you can afford to lose. RTO is how long you can afford to be down. Both need to be defined, documented, and tested — not assumed.

⏱️

RPO: Recovery Point Objective

With daily backups, your maximum data loss window is 24 hours. With continuous replication options, RPO can be reduced to minutes — critical for transaction-heavy environments.

🔄

RTO: Recovery Time Objective

How fast can you be back online after a failure? VulcanCloud's DR environment can bring up failed workloads in hours — not the days it takes to rebuild from scratch or wait on hardware replacements.

🛡️

Ransomware Protection

Immutable backup copies cannot be encrypted or deleted by ransomware — even if attackers have admin access to your environment. Your last clean backup is always recoverable.

📋

Compliance Documentation

Law firms, healthcare practices, and financial services businesses often need documented evidence of backup procedures. We provide the audit trail your compliance requirements demand.
