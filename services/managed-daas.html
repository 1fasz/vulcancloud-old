What Is It?

Your Complete Desktop in the Cloud

Desktop-as-a-Service (DaaS) moves your entire office desktop — every application, file, and setting — into a secure cloud environment delivered via Citrix, the industry gold standard for virtual desktop technology.

Your employees log in from any device — laptop, tablet, thin client — and get the same consistent experience. Data never lives on the endpoint. A lost or stolen device exposes nothing.

VulcanCloud manages the entire Citrix environment on your behalf — patching, performance monitoring, user provisioning, security configuration, and end-user support. You don't need a Citrix-certified engineer on staff.

Key Benefits

  • ✦Work securely from any device, anywhere
  • ✦No in-house Citrix expertise required
  • ✦Eliminate hardware refresh cycles
  • ✦Data never leaves the cloud environment
  • ✦New users onboard in minutes, not days
  • ✦Predictable per-user monthly cost
