What Is It?

Your Servers in the Cloud — Without the Overhead

Managed Cloud Servers replace the physical and virtual servers you run on-premise — or in an unmanaged colocation — with fully managed virtual machines running in enterprise US data centers.

We host and fully manage your line-of-business applications, SQL databases, file servers, and any other workloads that need a Windows or Linux server environment. You get predictable performance, proactive monitoring, and a team handling every patch and incident.

No more emergency hardware failures, no more deferred security patches, no more weekend emergency calls. VulcanCloud handles it all.

Key Benefits

  • ✦Eliminate hardware refresh cycles and CapEx
  • ✦Scale resources up or down as your needs change
  • ✦99.99% uptime SLA backed by US data centers
  • ✦24/7 monitoring — issues caught before you notice
  • ✦Automated backups with point-in-time recovery
  • ✦Predictable monthly cost — no surprise hardware bills

Who It's For

Is This the Right Fit?

Managed Cloud Servers are a strong fit if your business matches any of these profiles:

Running aging on-premise hardware

Your physical servers are past end-of-life or approaching it, and you'd rather not invest CapEx in new hardware — especially when cloud alternatives exist.

No dedicated IT staff

You don't have (or don't want) an in-house sysadmin responsible for patching, backups, and emergency response. We become your infrastructure team.

Compliance requirements

Your industry (legal, healthcare, financial) requires documented patching, access controls, audit logs, and a known-good backup posture. We deliver all of it.

Apps that can't move to SaaS

Some software requires a traditional server environment. We host it in the cloud without forcing you to adopt a SaaS model your workflow isn't ready for.
