What It Means

Proactive, Not Reactive

Most IT providers respond after something breaks. Your users call the helpdesk, the helpdesk opens a ticket, and someone investigates. Meanwhile, your team is down. This is reactive IT — and it's the standard most businesses accept without realizing it.

VulcanCloud's monitoring systems watch hundreds of metrics across your infrastructure in real time. When something drifts out of normal range — CPU pressure, storage utilization, authentication anomalies, performance degradation — our team gets the alert, not yours.

We investigate, diagnose, and resolve. You get a notification that we fixed something before it affected your team. That's proactive management.

What We Monitor

  • ✦Server CPU, memory, disk, and network utilization
  • ✦Virtual desktop session health and performance
  • ✦Security events and authentication anomalies
  • ✦Backup job completion and integrity checks
  • ✦Network connectivity, latency, and throughput
  • ✦Application availability and response times

What's Included

Every VulcanCloud managed environment includes comprehensive monitoring and management — not as an add-on, but as a core part of the service:

Monitoring & Alerting

  • Real-time infrastructure dashboards

  • Monthly performance reporting

Patch & Update Management

Incident Response

  • 24/7 on-call engineering coverage

Service Levels

Response Times You Can Count On

Our SLA tiers ensure that when something does need attention, you know exactly how fast we'll be on it.

🔴

Critical — 15 Min Response

Service-impacting outages affecting all users. Production environment down. All hands respond immediately with direct escalation to senior engineering.

🟠

High — 1 Hour Response

Degraded performance or partial service impact. Significant slowdowns, authentication issues, or single-system failures with workaround available.

🟡

Medium — 4 Hour Response

Non-critical issues affecting individual users or non-production systems. Software errors, configuration requests, and minor performance anomalies.

🟢

Low — Next Business Day

General requests, questions, user account changes, and non-urgent configuration modifications that don't affect active operations.
