What Is Backup & Disaster Recovery?

Your Last Line of Defense Against Data Loss

Every business faces the same risk: ransomware, hardware failure, accidental deletion, fire, or flood can destroy years of data in an instant. The question isn't whether you'll face a data loss event — it's whether you can recover when it happens.

VulcanCloud's backup and disaster recovery service automatically protects all your critical data and systems with encrypted cloud backups stored in our US-based private cloud. When disaster strikes, we restore your environment to the point just before the event — not to whenever your last manual backup happened.

For law firms and healthcare organizations, backup is also a compliance requirement. We provide the audit logging, retention policies, and encryption documentation your compliance framework demands.

Backup & DR at a Glance

  • ✦Automated daily (or more frequent) backups
  • ✦4-hour RTO for full environment recovery
  • ✦AES-256 encryption — data protected at all times
  • ✦Ransomware-resistant immutable backup storage
  • ✦Regular restore testing — not just backup creation
  • ✦Compliance-ready documentation & audit logs

How It Works

Set Up Once. Protected Continuously.

1

Data Inventory & Classification

We identify all critical data sources — servers, desktops, databases, email, and cloud files — and prioritize them by Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and Recovery Point Objective (RPO).

2

Backup Configuration

Automated backup schedules, retention policies, and encryption are configured for each data source. Backup jobs are validated before the service goes live.

3

Continuous Protection

Backups run automatically on schedule. VulcanCloud monitors backup job success and alerts our team to any failures — before you notice an issue.

4

Recovery on Demand

When you need a restore — whether it's a single file or a full environment — VulcanCloud executes the recovery quickly and keeps you informed throughout the process.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is data backed up and protected in VulcanCloud's cloud?

VulcanCloud performs automated daily backups of all client data, with more frequent intervals available for critical systems. All backup data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and stored in VulcanCloud's US-based private cloud — never on public cloud platforms. Backups are validated automatically and can be tested for restore integrity on demand.

How fast can VulcanCloud restore data after a disaster?

VulcanCloud backup and disaster recovery delivers Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) of 4 hours or less for full environment restores, and Recovery Point Objectives (RPO) as low as 1 hour for critical systems. Individual file and folder restores are typically completed within minutes via our self-service restore portal or by contacting our support team.

Does VulcanCloud backup support HIPAA compliance for healthcare organizations?

Yes. VulcanCloud's backup and disaster recovery is architected to support HIPAA compliance for healthcare covered entities and business associates. We provide encryption, access controls, audit logging, and Business Associate Agreements (BAA). PHI is backed up exclusively to VulcanCloud's isolated US-based private cloud, never commingled with other clients' data.

Can VulcanCloud replace our tape backup?

Yes. VulcanCloud cloud backup eliminates tape backup's physical media risk (theft, fire, flood), automates the entire backup process, and provides verified, tested restores that tape backup cannot guarantee. Most clients who switch from tape to VulcanCloud cloud backup reduce their recovery time from days to hours and eliminate backup management overhead entirely.

What happens to backup data if we leave VulcanCloud?

Your backup data belongs to you. VulcanCloud provides full data export in standard formats during any planned offboarding. We work with you to ensure zero data loss during any service transition and do not impose punitive data retention fees on clients who move their backup data elsewhere.

Does VulcanCloud test backup restores?

Yes. VulcanCloud performs automated backup integrity validation on all backup sets and offers scheduled restore tests for disaster recovery planning. Most businesses discover their backup has been silently failing only at the moment they need to restore — VulcanCloud's proactive testing ensures your backups are always recovery-ready before disaster strikes.
