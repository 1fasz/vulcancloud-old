Our Mission

Managed Infrastructure as a Service — Not a Project

Too many SMBs are running on a patchwork of aging on-premise servers, inconsistent remote access setups, and overworked IT generalists who are stretched across too many competing priorities. When something breaks, everything stops.

VulcanCloud was built to fix that. We take over the infrastructure layer entirely — virtual desktops, servers, backups, patching, monitoring, and support — and deliver it as an ongoing managed service. Not a one-time project. Not a break/fix arrangement. A running, monitored, supported environment with real accountability.

Our clients don't need to hire a Citrix-certified engineer or a dedicated sysadmin. They get that expertise through us — at a predictable monthly cost.

Infrastructure

US-based data centers with 99.99% uptime SLA. No offshore routing. Your data stays on domestic infrastructure.

Technology

Citrix-powered virtual desktops. XTIUM infrastructure partnership. Microsoft ecosystem expertise. Built on proven enterprise platforms.

Support Model

Dedicated engineers who know your environment — not a rotating help desk. Real accountability for the environments we manage.
