Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Home › Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

VulcanCloud.com is a managed cloud services provider based in Birmingham, Alabama, USA. We operate this website and provide managed Desktop-as-a-Service (DaaS), virtual desktop infrastructure, and managed cloud server solutions to businesses across the United States. Questions about this policy may be directed to us via the Contact page.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact form submissions (name, company, email, phone number, user count, message)
  • Email correspondence
  • Information provided during onboarding and service delivery

We may also collect standard web analytics data (page views, referral source, browser type, approximate geographic location) through third-party analytics services. This data is collected in aggregate and is not used to identify individual visitors.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotes
  • Deliver and manage cloud services to clients
  • Communicate about service status, updates, and renewals
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

All client data associated with managed service delivery is stored in US-based data centers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. For healthcare clients, we sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as required under HIPAA.

5. Cookies

This website may use cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. We do not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may link to third-party websites (such as Citrix or Microsoft partner pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Our managed cloud infrastructure is delivered through technology partnerships with Citrix and other enterprise platform providers; their respective privacy policies govern data processed through their platforms.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us through the Contact page. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe consistent with applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the Contact page. VulcanCloud operates from Birmingham, Alabama, USA.
