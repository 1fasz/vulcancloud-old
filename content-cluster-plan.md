## Publication Cadence
- **Month 1:** Posts 1, 4, 7 (one per cluster, foundation content)
- **Month 2:** Posts 2, 5, 8, 10, 13 (build out each cluster)
- **Month 3:** Posts 3, 6, 9, 11, 14 (deepen clusters)
- **Month 4:** Posts 12, 15 + performance review and gap analysis

## Internal Linking Rules
1. Every post links back to its pillar page at least once.
2. Every post links to one related service page (DaaS, Backup, etc.).
3. Pillar pages link to their 3 supporting posts in a "Related Reading" section.
4. Posts cross-link to adjacent clusters where topics overlap.

## Schema Notes
- Add `Article` JSON-LD to each blog post (headline, datePublished, author, publisher).
- Add `FAQPage` JSON-LD to posts that include Q&A sections.
- Add `HowTo` JSON-LD to step-by-step posts (migration guides, checklists).
