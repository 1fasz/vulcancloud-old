How We Price

Why We Quote Instead of List

Managed cloud services aren't a commodity — two companies with the same user count can have vastly different infrastructure requirements, compliance needs, and application complexity. Publishing a grid of dollar amounts would mean you're overpaying for resources you don't need, or undershooting on what we actually deliver.

Instead, every VulcanCloud engagement starts with a discovery conversation. We assess what you have, what you need, and what your environment requires — then build a quote around that reality. No hidden fees. No surprise invoices. Predictable monthly billing.

Pricing Factors

What Goes Into Your Quote

These are the variables that shape your monthly investment:

👥

Number of Users

Virtual desktop and user-license costs scale directly with your seat count. More users typically means better per-user economics.

🖥️

Compute Requirements

CPU and RAM allocations are matched to your workloads — light office work vs. compute-intensive applications require different sizing.

📦

Storage & Data Volume

User profile storage, database volume, and backup retention windows all affect your monthly storage cost.

🔒

Compliance Needs

HIPAA-aligned or legal-industry configurations may require additional security controls, audit logging, or documentation.

🛠️

Application Complexity

Custom application publishing, specialized software licensing, or legacy app compatibility can affect setup and monthly management.

📞

Support Level

Business-hours support vs. 24/7 end-user helpdesk vs. dedicated account engineer — your required coverage model shapes the cost.
